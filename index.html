Food for thought

Ideas are meant to be shared. At every session, we discuss a special climate issue while enjoying tea and chocolate every Wednesday at 5:00pm. Everyone is encouraged to take part in the conversation. Can carbon technologies really help? What is the future of food? Is degrowth the solution? In case you are not inspired, we provide you with our "food for thought", a list of resources to be well prepared ! You will discover lots of fun anecdotes, statistics, ads, images...
